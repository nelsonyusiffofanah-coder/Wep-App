Your data stays encrypted on your devices. JSONBin is just a secure storage bridge.
📱 First Device
💻 Other Device
3 steps — takes 3 minutes:
1. Go to jsonbin.io → Sign Up free
2. Click your Profile → API Keys → Copy the Secret Key
3. Paste it below and tap Connect
👁
Your key never leaves your device — it is encrypted with your data.
✅ Connected! Your data is now syncing.
To sync on your other device (phone/PC), copy this Bin ID:
Then open the app on your other device → Settings → Cloud Sync → Other Device tab
You already connected on your first device. Now enter the same Secret Key + the Bin ID you copied.
